Hot Beans Web is web developer training company that was founded on October 5th, 2005 by Adrian Lim, a graduate of Stansfield college, Singapore, where he studied Computer Science and currently holds a doctoral degree in his field of study. Following his graduation, Mr Adrian decided to found this small to medium company with the aim of utilising the skills of already proficient web developers to solve everyday software/online problems of people. Its first store was based in Kampong Glam, Singapore where it saw to the needs of people in the area.

It was then moved to London in 2012 to further maximise the potential of the team at the store. Hot Beans Web developed a reputation for being able to meet its customers' needs proficiently and efficiently. The company is adored by its customer base and is seeking to employ developers, maximising their potential and utilising their skills to further grow the company and improve the customer base results.

Hot Beans Web takes their customers seriously, treating them like family and ensuring their problems are solved in the best way possible under a short period of time. They also take their employees seriously, ensuring a good working condition and providing top notch equipment needed to work in the company.

Aim Of Hot Beans Web

  • To attract and use the skills of junior and senior web developers
  • To maximise the benefit of using software, IT technology and online services
  • To improve interaction between potential and existing customers
  • To become the no 1 web service providers in London
  • To become a recognised web service provider around the world
  • To help children who leave school, giving them a second chance
